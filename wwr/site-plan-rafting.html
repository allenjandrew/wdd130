Overview

Purpose

We've stayed away from the nets for too long. This website is to establish our company's presence online. The site will provide information about our company and things such as the river's history, rafting safety, and things to do nearby. The site will also provide a platform for clients to make rafting and gear reservations, pay, and leave reviews. The site will convey a feeling of rugged excitement tempered by homestyle comfort.

Audience

Our audience consists mainly of adults and young adults ages 20 to 40. Many of clients are experienced rafters, and just want a seamless, traditional experience. Many others are new or inexperienced rafters, who need to feel safe and informed. We also get many groups of Scouts and youth groups. We can expect this site to be accessed primarily on desktop, but also on mobile.
