Overview

Purpose

Think about your kitchen. How many recipe books do you have on the shelf, cluttering up space? If the answer is more than zero, you might be interested in a personal website where you can store all your own recipes - a site that makes it easy to upload, view, and share your favorite family recipes at any time and from anywhere. My goal is to create a clean and simple-to-use website where you can do just that.

Audience

Anybody who does anything in the kitchen!
